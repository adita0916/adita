Walmart Sales

Objectives

Through this project, I am aiming to answer:
- Which month had the highest sales in each year?
- How was the weather during the month of highest sale?
- Do the sales always rise near the holiday season for all the years?

Tools

MySQL was used to explore the dataset.

Data

The dataset was Walmart Sales Forecast dataset taken from Kaggle. For the purpose of this project, train and features csv files were used. https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/aslanahmedov/walmart-sales-forecast

Which month had the highest sales in each year?

December had the highest sales for two consecutive years.

How was the weather during the month of highest sale?

If the temperature was above 72 Fahrenheit, it was labelled as hot.
If the temperature was between 40 and 72 Fahrenheit, it was labelled as mild.
If the temperature was below 40 Fahrenheit, it was labeled as cold.
During the best sales of the month, the weather was consistently mild.

Do the sales always rise near the holiday season for all the years?

The sales are usually better when it is not on holidays. The gap was about 15 million dollars. In 2011, the gap was 16.9M.
